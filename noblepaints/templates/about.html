Our Vision

Noble Paints was established in 1991 AD, and in a short period of time, and as a result of the vision and dedication of the founders, the efforts have succeeded in laying strong grounds for a leading company in the paint industry in GCC countries and the Middle East in general. Hence, the company became a leading company in manufacturing and developing innovative high quality and eco-friendly paints designed to meet the requirements and needs of a wide range of clients including industrial, commercial, and individuals

Over the next five years, we aim to become a leading force in paints & coatings industry on the premise of adopting a specific sector (Segment by sector) marketing strategy to develop Saudi and regional markets. The brand equity & the reputation we have built over the last four decades drives us further to the future in ensuring our defined goals & objectives.The company's success stems from a deeper understanding of local market trends, varied customers' needs fueled with exceptional customer service that enables us to meet each paints & coatings requirement.
We respect the environment and in order to make the workplace & our planet a healthier and safer place we go beyond our ways to complying with global legislation on health and safety issues.

The Vision of Noble paints is to become a global leader in innovative painting, coating, and construction solution, and the most reliable partner to customers in the market.
