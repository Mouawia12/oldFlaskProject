Research &
Development

Besides conducting on- going research, with our international specialists, our original paints innovations have continually redefined the paint industry, at the same time adding value to life and the environment through our complete paint and coating solutions.
At any given time Noble evaluates between 10 and 20 products for areas of possible product improvement. A typical year sees the development of at least five new products and the upgrading about 5 to 15 products. This constant innovation and improvement has played a key role in the success of brand Noble. Our research & technical team along with experienced and qualified chemists constantly analyze the application, finish, aesthetics, and longevity and service requirement of our clients

Quality Control

Noble Quality Standards are at the core.The quality assurance comes from various key aspects of the framework. Stakeholders are encouraged to work towards continuous improvement through engagement in the processes of planning and evaluation,
All Quality Control (QC) activities are carried out in accordance with ISO standards and rules of the authorities and International standards such as ASTM (American Standard) BS (British Standard) & SASO (Saudi Standard), safety regulations and company policies. The laboratory ensures consistent quality, relevant tests are conducted periodically ensuring results are well within the set standards. QC also monitors raw materials, shelf life, stability and packaging in order to make sure it is within specifications and under control led conditions.
The Research & Development and Quality Control labs are equipped with the most modern equipment to maintain the quality bench marks that have become the hallmark of Noble products.
